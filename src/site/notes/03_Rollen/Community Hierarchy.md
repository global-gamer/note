# Community Hierarchy (English Titles)

Struktur für Discord-Rollen, Permission-Groups und Onboarding. Alle Titel orientieren sich an etablierten Gaming-Communities und eSports-Organisationen.

## Executive Council (Equal Level)
- **Operations Director (IT & Staff)** – Owns infrastructure, staff ops & tooling ([[03_Rollen/Projektleitungen#Operations Director (IT & Staff)\|Details]]).
- **Growth Director (Sales & Marketing)** – Leads sponsorships, partnerships, content & donations ([[03_Rollen/Projektleitungen#Growth Director (Sales & Marketing)\|Projektleitungen#Growth Director (Sales & Marketing)]]).
- **Community Director (Community & Events)** – Drives player experience, events & moderation ([[03_Rollen/Projektleitungen#Community Director (Community & Events)\|Projektleitungen#Community Director (Community & Events)]]).

## Division: Operations Network
- **Operations Engineer (Tech Lead)** – Servers, bots, automation ([[04_Infrastruktur/Server Setup\|Server Setup]]).
- **Support Captain** – Tickets, bans, escalations ([[03_Rollen/Moderation und Support Roles\|Moderation und Support Roles]]).
- **Staff Coordinator** – Rosters, training, recruiting.
- **Automation & Bot Squad** – Bot Devs, tooling specialists.

## Division: Growth Collective
- **Growth Manager (Media Lead)** – Content calendar, social drops ([[04_Infrastruktur/Plattformen\|Plattformen]]).
    - **Partnerships Manager** – Sponsorship pipeline, partner care ([[05_Phasenplan/Phase 7 Monetization\|Phase 7 Monetization]]).
- **Merch & VIP Manager** – Supporter perks, shops, loyalty ([[02_Ziele/Finanzielle Ziele\|Finanzielle Ziele]]).
- **Creator Relations Lead** – Liaison to streamers, content partners.
- **Data & Insights Analyst** – KPI dashboards, campaign reporting.

## Division: Community Experience Guild
- **Community Manager** – Rules, onboarding, culture ([[05_Phasenplan/Phase 1 Infrastructure Build\|Phase 1 Infrastructure Build]], [[05_Phasenplan/Phase 2 Culture Pilot\|Phase 2 Culture Pilot]]).
- **Event Director** – Event roadmap, execution, retros ([[Templates/Event-Template\|Templates/Event-Template]]).
- **Moderation Captains** – Lead mod squads per focus game.
- **Onboarding Guides** – Manage applications, welcome journeys.

## Moderation & Support Ladder
- **Senior Moderator** – Coaches mod team, handles escalations.
- **Moderator** – Day-to-day chat/voice moderation, tickets, event support.
- **Helper / Scout** – First touch for guests, feedback gatherer, community pulse.

## Partner & Supporter Groups
- **Founders Circle** – Strategic donors & long-term backers supporting infra costs.
- **Supporter Guild (VIP)** – Monthly supporters with cosmetic perks & private lounges.
- **Creator Alliance** – Streamers / content partners boosting reach with co-branded events.
- **Partner Collective** – Brands/hosts providing hardware, servers or prize pools.
- **Community Ambassadors** – Trusted members who represent player voice in councils.

## Community Ranks
- **Member** – Full community access, eligible for events and squads.
- **Guest / Trial** – New joiners, guided by Helpers until onboarding is complete.
- **Legacy / Alumni** – Inaktive Mitglieder mit Ehrenstatus (optional Role für Rückkehrer).

> Map diese Rollen direkt auf Discord Permission Groups und rücke in Onboarding-Docs sowie [[03_Rollen/Moderation und Support Roles\|Moderation und Support Roles]] immer auf diese Struktur, damit Reporting-Linien klar bleiben.
