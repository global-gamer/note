# Community Hierarchy (English Titles)

Struktur für Discord-Rollen, Permission-Groups und Onboarding. Alle Titel orientieren sich an etablierten Gaming-Communities und eSports-Organisationen.

## Executive Council (Equal Level)
- **Ops Captain (IT & Crew)** – Owns infrastructure, staff ops & tooling ([[03_Rollen/Projektleitungen#Ops Captain (IT & Crew)\|Details]]).
- **Growth Captain (Sales & Marketing)** – Leads sponsorships, partnerships, content & donations ([[03_Rollen/Projektleitungen#Growth Captain (Sales & Marketing)\|Projektleitungen#Growth Captain (Sales & Marketing)]]).
- **Community Captain (Community & Events)** – Drives player experience, events & moderation ([[03_Rollen/Projektleitungen#Community Captain (Community & Events)\|Projektleitungen#Community Captain (Community & Events)]]).

## Division: Operations Network
- **Server Pilot (Tech Lead)** – Servers, bots, automation ([[04_Infrastruktur/Server Setup\|Server Setup]]).
- **Player Support Lead** – Tickets, bans, escalations ([[03_Rollen/Moderation und Support Roles\|Moderation und Support Roles]]).
- **Crew Coordinator** – Rosters, training, recruiting.
- **Automation & Bot Squad** – Bot Devs, tooling specialists.

## Division: Growth Collective
- **Content Captain** – Content calendar, social drops ([[04_Infrastruktur/Plattformen\|Plattformen]]).
    - **Partner Scout** – Sponsorship pipeline, partner care ([[05_Phasenplan/Phase 8 Monetization\|Phase 8 Monetization]]).
- **Supporter Lead** – Supporter perks, shops, loyalty ([[02_Ziele/Finanzielle Ziele\|Finanzielle Ziele]]).
- **Creator Liaison** – Liaison to streamers, content partners.
- **Insights Guide** – KPI dashboards, campaign reporting.

## Division: Community Experience Guild
- **Community Host** – Rules, onboarding, culture ([[05_Phasenplan/Phase 2 Infrastructure Build\|Phase 2 Infrastructure Build]], [[05_Phasenplan/Phase 3 Culture Pilot\|Phase 3 Culture Pilot]]).
- **Event Producer** – Event roadmap, execution, retros ([[Templates/Event-Template\|Templates/Event-Template]]).
- **Guardian Leads** – Lead mod squads per focus game.
- **Onboarding Guides** – Manage applications, welcome journeys.

## Guardian & Support Ladder
- **Senior Guardian** – Coaches mod team, handles escalations.
- **Guardian** – Day-to-day chat/voice moderation, tickets, event support.
- **Welcome Guide** – First touch for guests, feedback gatherer, community pulse.

## Partner & Supporter Groups
- **Founders Circle** – Strategic donors & long-term backers supporting infra costs.
- **Supporter Guild (VIP)** – Monthly supporters with cosmetic perks & private lounges.
- **Creator Alliance** – Streamers / content partners boosting reach with co-branded events.
- **Partner Collective** – Brands/hosts providing hardware, servers or prize pools.
- **Community Ambassadors** – Trusted members who represent player voice in councils.

## Community Ranks
- **Member** – Full community access, eligible for events and squads.
- **Guest / Trial** – New joiners, guided by Welcome Guides until onboarding is complete.
- **Legacy / Alumni** – Inaktive Mitglieder mit Ehrenstatus (optional Role für Rückkehrer).

> Map diese Rollen direkt auf Discord Permission Groups und rücke in Onboarding-Docs sowie [[03_Rollen/Moderation und Support Roles\|Moderation und Support Roles]] immer auf diese Struktur, damit Reporting-Linien klar bleiben.
