# Creator Labs Playbook – Hypothese

Programm für Creator-Booster, Workshops und Co-Creation. Ziel: Sponsoring-Pipeline beschleunigen, Content-Schleifen stärken und Nachwuchs-Creator fördern.

## Zielsetzung
- **Phase Gate:** [[05_Phasenplan/Phase 7 Growth Sprint\|Phase 7 Growth Sprint]] (Pilot) → Skalierung in [[05_Phasenplan/Phase 8 Monetization\|Phase 8 Monetization]].
- **KPI Targets:** 4 Creator-Kollabs/Quartal, +2 aktive Sponsoren, Social Reach +25 %.
- **Owners:** Creator Manager (Lead), Partnerships Manager, Growth Director.

## Programmdesign
1. **Recruitment Funnel**
   - Bewerbungen via Landingpage + Discord Channel `#creator-labs`.
   - Kriterien: Reichweite (min. 5k Follower), Community Fit, Content-Stil passend zu [[01_Vision/Zielgruppe\|Zielgruppe]].
   - Intake-Board (Trello/Notion) mit Scorecard (Reach, Engagement, Professionalism).
2. **Bootcamp Weeks (4 Wochen)**
   - Woche 1: Onboarding, Brand Guidelines ([[04_Infrastruktur/Domains und Branding\|Domains und Branding]]).
   - Woche 2: Content Lab – Templates, Motion Graphics, Co-Streaming Sessions.
   - Woche 3: Sponsorship 101 – Media Kit, CTA, Taschengeld Funnel Storyline.
   - Woche 4: Launch Sprint – Creator veröffentlicht min. 2 Inhalte, 1 Event-Host-Rolle.
3. **Perks & Support**
   - Dedicated Producer/Editor Slot.
   - Mini-Grant (z. B. 250 € Equipment) gegen Content Commitment.
   - Creator-only Feedback Forum + Access zu Growth Analytics.

## Monetarisierung & Sponsoring
- **Partner Packages:** „Creator Labs Presented by X“ mit gebrandeten Assets.
- **Rev Share:** Sponsoring Deals → 10–20 % Kickback an Creator, Rest in Community Fund.
- **Shop Integration:** Limited Merch Drops mit Creator Branding (siehe Playbook Backlog).

## Ops & Tooling
- Notion/Trello Board für Pipeline und Tasking.
- Content Storage (Google Drive/Lumen5) mit Template Packs.
- Weekly sync via Discord Stage + recorded sessions.
- Reporting: Data Studio Dashboard (Views, CTA, Sponsoring leads) → Growth Council.

## Risks & Mitigation
- **Brand Misalignment:** Strenges Screening + Code of Conduct.
- **Burnout/Dropout:** Mini-stipends + mental-health check-ins.
- **Sponsor Conflicts:** Verträge mit Approval Clause; Growth Director final approval.
