# IRL Pop-up Plan

Mini-Events / Meetups zur Community-Bindung und Sponsor-Aktivierung. Dient als Brücke zwischen digitaler Community und physischen Erlebnissen.

## Ziele
- Loyalty steigern, VIP/Supporter belohnen, Creator <> Member zusammenbringen.
- Sponsoren greifbare Aktivierung bieten (Brand Booths, Hardware Demos).
- Medienpräsenz aufbauen (lokale Presse, Streams).
- Phase Gate: [[05_Phasenplan/Phase 6 Stabilize Automate\|Phase 6 Stabilize Automate]] (Pilot) → Skalierung in [[05_Phasenplan/Phase 9 Expansion\|Phase 9 Expansion]].

## Konzeptbausteine
1. **Formattypen**
   - Coffee Meetups (20–30 Member) – Fokus auf Feedback, Networking.
   - Pop-up LAN / Viewing Party (50–100 Personen) – Turniere, Showmatches.
   - Creator Showcase (Hybrid) – Live-Podcast, Workshops, Merch Drop.
2. **Programmpunkte**
   - Welcome + Manifest Reminder (Heritage Story, [[00_Meta/Manifest\|00_Meta/Manifest]]).
   - Panels/Workshops (Guardian Lead + Creator).
   - Taschengeld Funnel Demo / Supporter Perks (opt-in).
   - Partner Booths (Hardware, ISP, Merch).

## Ops & Logistics
- **Location**: Coworking Space / eSports Bar / Pop-up Store.
- **Budget**: 3–5k € pro Event (Location, Technik, Catering, Travel).
- **Team Roles**:
  - Event Producer (Owner)
  - Server Pilot (Tech Setup, streaming)
  - Community Host (Onsite host)
  - Creator Liaison (talent wrangling)
- **Timeline**:
  - T-8 Wochen: Location sichern, Sponsoren pitch.
  - T-6 Wochen: Ticketing (Eventbrite/Discord), Promo Plan.
  - T-2 Wochen: Final production run-through, volunteer briefings.
  - T-0: Execution + Social Coverage.
  - T+1 Woche: Recap, KPI review, lessons to Manifest.

## KPIs
- Attendance Rate ≥ 80 % (Tickets zu actual).
- ≥2 Sponsoren aktiv am Event.
- Content Output: 3 Clips, 1 Recap Article.
- Feedback Score (NPS) ≥ 60.

## Risks & Mitigation
- **Kostenüberschreitung**: Budget-Trackers + Sponsor-Shoring.
- **No-Show**: Waitlist + deposit-based tickets.
- **Compliance**: Haftung, Versicherungen, Foto-Consent Form.
- **Health/Safety**: Risk Assessment, Onsite Ops Captain.
